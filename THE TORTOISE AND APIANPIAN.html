THE TORTOISE AND APIANPIAN

Once upon a time there was in animal kingdom a bird called Apianpian and Mr tortoise and the kingdom was living quietly and peacefully until a Famine hit the kingdom, it was so severe that so many animals died and many moved to another far distance kingdom. Tortoise and Apianpian were very good family friend, for tortoise and his family, the faming was terrible they can stay for days without food and his children were falling sick seriously for lack of food but for Apianpian, they eat every time, her family were enjoying food like other time when there was no faming, tortoise did not know how his friend is managing her family as such a hard time, one day he decided to ask his friend, he said “please my friend I and my family is dying of starvation , we have nothing to eat, we don’t have anywhere to go, can you tell me how you are getting food for your family in this hard time”? Apianpian smile and said “ is a long story” please my dear friend I am ready to listen provided I get any food for my family said tortoise, the place is somewhere beyond the sky and its impossible for featherless animals to go there; ah what are friend for? You can help me said tortoise, Ok , I always go very early in the morning, tomorrow morning I will come and call you so we can go together said Apianpian, ok then thank you very much my good friend replied Tortoise . It was early in the morning, tortoise wake up, get ready and wait patiently for His friend, mean while, Apianpian have gone without calling tortoise because for Apianpian tortoise is not a good friend, and she did not want to help him; tortoise waited and waited but his friend did not come, he decided to go check for his friend when he got there, APianpian has gone, tortoise came back disappointed , in the evening tortoise went to his friend house and meet them eating but Apianpian come outside to respond to him, never want tortoise to know the type of food they were eating, she said please my friend I am very sorry I forgot to call you this morning tomorrow is another day. The next day tortoise wake before 4 o’clock AM and crawn to Apianpian house when he got there, he located her bag and enter inside quietly as soon as Apiapian wake up she took her bag not knowing tortoise was hiding inside and be on her way beyond the sky without calling tortoise to come with her, as she arrived there, she started harvesting the palm fruit as she put them on her bag, tortoise will eat them and throw the kernel away, after some time Apianpian usese her hand to feel the bag and said what is happening I have been putting so many fruit into this bag still I never feel the weight, so she decided to put her hand inside , don’t put hand on my eyes! Said tortoise, did you called my now? Why are you doing this to me? I am very sorry my friend just that I cant just understand how I will bring you here seeing that you have no feather, now come out and stay the other side once I finish I will put you on my back because I want to used my bag for the fruit said Apiapian, No! I cant you will surely leave me here replied tortoise,

no I cant do that to you, ok let me give you one feather to hold so that you will know I am still here, ok but Apianpian plucked off her feather and pretended that it still connected to her but unknowingly to tortoise it has been plucked off from his friend, so tortoise agreed and move to the other side while he hold on to the feather, after some time tortoise called on his Friend “Apianpian Apianpian are you not hearing me ? but there was no respond he called again and again but Apianpian has gone, tortoise decided to move around to check for his friend surprisingly the feather was plucked to his hand, Oh! I am finish said tortoise, how Will I get down from here? Who will help me? As he began to talked with himself behold Mr Dove come around , Dove said what is this wicked and cunny man is doing here, how did he managed to get here? Before now , Tortoise and Dove have been having issues between them because of land , they are neighbour but not good one; When tortoise saw him, he said to himself , let me beg him to have mercy on me and take a word to my family , Now tortoise said to Dove “ my Neighbour you come here? Dove replied yes and you, what are you doing here? Only animal with feather come here how managed did you find your way here? I know you must have deceived someone to bring you here, as cunny as you are you can do anything for everything, not so my friend it was APianpian that brought me here but now she has left me please I need your help, me, your friend? Are you joking? What help can you need from me? Tortoise answer and said please go to my house tell my family to bring all the foam and every soft things in our home and bring them under this place once they are done, bring me a word so that I can release myself from here, ok replied Mr Dove, I will do as you said he departed. When Dove get Home he told Tortoise’s family that their father said they should bring axes, stones, cutlass rocks and every hard things they can laid their hands; so they did as he said and Mr dove return to Tortoise and said they are done everything is ready, oh thank you for this said Tortoise; Now Tortoise releases himself from the sky unfortunately he landed on the solid things his family was wrongly told to prepared for him and his shell shattered and he shouted what have you done? Ah these were what Mr Dove told us said his wife, oh he mislead you; After that Tortoise family rebuild his shell but with rough pattern that was how Tortoise end up with lines and rough pattern till this day.
