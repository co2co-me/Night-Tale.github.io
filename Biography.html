About

Pastor Felix Itaman Uwamusi hail from Ewatto town in Esan South East Local Government Area of Edo state Nigeria in west Africa, he studied computing with information system in saint martins institute of higher learning Malta, he also hold national diploma in computer engineer in federal polytechnic oko, also CCNA Certification in computer networking. He is a java and python programmer, a web designer and Data analyst, an Internet security expert; he is the Author of "JESUS AND THE LAW" a Christian hand book, is a pastor in omega fire ministries Malta Branch, a husband and father. the owner of thevillagegist.com a stories and online news page.
